About Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is baked in an oven until crispy and golden, making it a versatile and delicious meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Dry Yeast
  • Warm Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, sugar, flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead, let rise 1 hour.
  2. Mix tomato sauce with herbs and seasoning.
  3. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C), roll out dough.
  4. Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-15 mins until golden and bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
